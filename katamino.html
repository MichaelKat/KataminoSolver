Katamino Solver

Katamino is a puzzle game in which the player is given a board of a certain size and a set of pieces to fill that board. Each piece is unique in shape but consists of 5 square "blocks", meaning all pieces have the same area. When it comes to the board, even though the height of the board is a constant 5 blocks, its width depends on the number of pieces selected; for each piece selected, the board gets one block added to its width. Thus, the board always has just enough space to accomodate all the pieces selected - the challenge is to make them all fit!
